The dataset contains 150 records with no missing values or duplicates, making it clean and reliable for analysis.
The target variable is perfectly balanced, with each Iris species having exactly 50 samples.
Petal length and petal width show greater variability compared to sepal measurements.
Iris-setosa is clearly distinguishable from the other species, while Iris-versicolor and Iris-virginica overlap slightly.
Strong positive correlation exists between petal length and petal width, indicating their importance in classification.
